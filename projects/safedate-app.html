The Project

What it is about

Meeting people from the internet is normal now. But knowing they are who they say they are is a different story. SafeDate is a mobile app concept that lets users verify someone before a first meetup, with an SOS alert for emergencies and a premium tier for extra peace of mind.

My role

I took care of the full UX design process, from mapping user flows and information architecture all the way to high-fidelity screens and an interactive Figma prototype. I also documented the safety goals and design decisions as I went.

Process

How I got there

Looking Back

What I learned

This one pushed me to think harder about safety-critical UX. When someone might actually be in danger, every label, tap area, and button size carries real weight. Accessibility is not an afterthought here. It is the whole point.
