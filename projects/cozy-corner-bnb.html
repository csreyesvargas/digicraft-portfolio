The Project

What it is about

Cozy Corner B&B needed a website that could actually turn browsers into guests. The design had to show off the rooms in a way that felt personal, make the booking process simple, and build enough trust that a first-time visitor would feel good about clicking that reserve button.

My role

I designed the full multi-page site in Figma, including the homepage, rooms page with carousel, full booking flow, dining section, and account pages, all connected in an interactive prototype.

Process

How I got there

1

Research

Looked at hospitality sites and mapped out where booking flows fall apart.

2

IA and Flows

Planned the site structure and mapped the end-to-end booking journey.

3

Visual Design

Warm typography, room carousel, and a clean calendar UI.

4

Prototype

Full Figma prototype: browse a room, pick dates, book, and confirm.

5

End-to-end usability test on the booking and account flows.

Looking Back

What I learned

Hospitality design is really about trust. The typography, photos, and flow all have to work together to make someone comfortable handing over their card and showing up somewhere new. Small details carry a lot of weight here.
