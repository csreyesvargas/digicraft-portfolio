Project Overview

The Problem

Cozy Corner B&B needed a website that could convert browsers into bookers — presenting rooms beautifully, making the booking flow intuitive, and building trust with first-time visitors. The design needed to feel warm and personal, not corporate.

My Role

Designed the full multi-page website in Figma — homepage, rooms page with carousel, booking flow, dining page, and account pages — with an end-to-end interactive prototype.

Design Process

How I Got There

Reflection

What I Learned

Hospitality design is all about trust — the visual design, copy, and flow all need to work together to make someone feel comfortable handing over their credit card and showing up somewhere new. Typography and spacing decisions I might have glossed over elsewhere mattered a lot here.
