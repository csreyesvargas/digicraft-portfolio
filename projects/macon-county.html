Project Overview

The Problem

Macon County needed a website that clearly communicated what the county offers — from local history to services and notable places like the "Lost Towns" of the region. The challenge was organizing dense information into a clean, navigable structure that worked for a general audience.

My Role

I designed and built the entire website from scratch — writing all HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, implementing Bootstrap for responsive layout, and deploying the live site via FileZilla. This is the only fully coded project in my portfolio.

Reflection

What I Learned

Building a site from scratch is a completely different discipline than designing in Figma — you're accountable for every pixel, every broken link, and every line of code. This project built my confidence as a developer and showed me how much UX thinking goes into seemingly small decisions like nav labels and page titles.
