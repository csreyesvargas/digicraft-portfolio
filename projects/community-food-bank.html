The Project

What it is about

The original food bank website made it hard for people to find what they needed quickly. Volunteer sign-ups were buried, the donation flow was confusing, and the information was hard to scan. This redesign focused on making those three things fast and simple.

My role

Full UX process from research through high-fidelity prototype. I ran usability tests, iterated on the findings, and documented everything in a complete case study.

Looking Back

What I learned

Someone visiting a food bank website is often already stressed. That context changed how I thought about every design decision. Clarity is not just nice to have here. It is the whole job.
