Project Overview

The Problem

The existing Community Food Bank website made it difficult for community members to find resources, volunteer, or donate quickly. The information architecture was unclear, the visual design was outdated, and key calls to action were buried.

My Role

Full UX process from research through high-fidelity prototype — including usability testing, iterating on findings, and writing a complete case study documenting the design decisions.

Reflection

What I Learned

This project taught me how important clarity is for users who are already in a stressful situation — someone visiting a food bank website may be anxious or in need. Every word, button, and layout decision carries weight.
