About Me

Designing with purpose.
Building with care.

I'm a Web & UX Design student based in Raleigh, NC, focused on creating digital experiences that are clean, accessible, and genuinely useful. My work sits at the intersection of design thinking and front-end development.

I believe good design is invisible — it just works. Whether I'm mapping a user flow in Figma or writing HTML from scratch, I'm always thinking about the person on the other side of the screen.
